Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 21, 2026

Overview

Don't Drop the Cat is an offline single-player puzzle game. We do not require account registration, and we do not collect personal information through the game.

Information We Collect

We do not collect personal data such as your name, email address, phone number, contacts, photos, or location.

The app stores gameplay settings and local progress on your device only, such as unlocked levels, audio preferences, and completion status.

How Information Is Used

Local save data is used only to support gameplay features, including restoring progress, remembering settings, and improving the in-app experience for the current device.

Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share personal information with third parties because the game does not collect personal information in the first place.

Internet and Third-Party Services

The game is designed to work offline and does not require a network connection for gameplay. It does not include account systems, online matchmaking, or third-party advertising SDKs.

Children's Privacy

Don't Drop the Cat is intended to be family-friendly. Since no personal data is collected, the app does not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Data Retention

Progress and settings remain stored on your device until you delete the app or clear the app's local data through system tools.

Contact

If you have privacy-related questions, you can contact us at: support@dontdropthecat.example
