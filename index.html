Don't Drop the Cat

A soft and colorful puzzle game where every swipe moves the whole board. Merge matching cats, avoid dangerous pits, and solve 50 relaxing logic challenges designed for quick sessions on iPhone and iPad.

Why Players Like It

Simple to Learn

Swipe in any direction and every cat slides together. The controls are immediate and friendly even for casual players.

Thoughtful Puzzles

Color pits, tight layouts, and matching rules turn each board into a compact logic problem instead of a random swipe game.

Relaxing Mood

Soft colors, cute cat art, and gentle audio feedback keep the game calm while the puzzle difficulty gradually increases.

Gameplay Highlights

Match the Right Cats

Bring identical cats together with carefully planned swipes. One wrong route can block the board or send a cat into danger.

Avoid Wrong Pits

Colored pits create risk. You need to read the whole board, not just the next move.

Play Anywhere

No internet, no login, and no account setup. Progress is saved locally so the game is always ready for a quick session.
